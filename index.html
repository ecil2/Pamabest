September 12th - 19th

Interested in Pop? Then make your way to the first leg of the festival. With names of fame in the modern Pop genre featured, you are guaranteed an unforgettable epxerience.
What with the Pamabest festival's first run beginning in Paris' very own Bois de Vincennes, one could even bring their better half without a hitch.

Drum‘n’bass will be the second genre, this time in sunny Spain! This particular segment of your Pamabest experience will be taking part in the beautiful Retiro Park; situated in the heart of Madrid and indeed Spain itself.
Amongst the Drum‘n’bass bonanza there will be performers such as Pendulum, Modestep & Sub Focus.

Third in the stack of genres the Pamabest festival has to offer is Metal. If you are a Metal fanatic then make your way to the city of darkness.
This part of the festival will be situated in the massive (in fame as well as landmass) Tiergarten Park in central Berlin.
We at Pamabest hope to see you there!

Pamabest Festival completes its final leg in the northern city of Stockholm, Sweden.
If you haven’t seen Sweden yet then this is the opportune time to not only see Jazz Legends perform their favourite compositions, but also the sould of music.
See Miles Davis, Pee Wee Ellis & Matt Bianco chill the crowd with music.
